**Pie Chart**
The pie chart, created using data from the `GOOGLE/google_maps_search_3.json` file, illustrates the proportional distribution of different **business types** identified in the dataset. Each slice of the chart represents a unique business category, and its size reflects the relative number of businesses falling under that type. This visualization provides a quick overview of the most common business categories based on Google Maps search results.

**Bar Chart**
The bar chart, created using data from the `GOOGLE/google_maps_search_3.json` file, displays the **number of businesses** grouped by their respective **types**. Each bar represents a specific business category, and its height corresponds to the total number of entries classified under that type. This visualization offers a clear comparison of how frequently different business types appear in the dataset based on Google Maps search results.
